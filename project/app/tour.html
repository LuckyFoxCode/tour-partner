Overview

Every room and suite at Conrad Bali is designed to make your stay memorable, easy and intimate. Located along the southern coast of Bali, about 30 minutes from Ngurah Rai International Airport (DPS), guests can select from ocean-view rooms, Conrad suites with their own infinity pools, or Conrad Pool Villa with a private plunge pool, steam room, as well as an outdoor bathtub and cozy bale with Balinese touch. Stay inspired at The Reflection Lounge, where guests can stay later or arrive earlier; furnished with showers, refreshments and complimentary WiFi connection, serves as an extended-stay base. Keep connected with WiFi and experience our award-winning resort’s leisure facilities, fitness and spa. The resort's restaurants and bars serve a variety of delicacies, including: • Alfresco beachfront and BBQ dining at Eight Degrees South • RIN Japanese cuisine with an international twist • Azure Bar and Kiosk overlooking the Indian Ocean • East Lobby Lounge's coffees and cocktails The cultural immersion experience of Fire Kecak dance on sunset at Uluwatu Temple, visual tour of Indonesia's culture and diversity at Devdan Theater, Bali Golf Country Club and shops of Bali Collection are only a short drive away.
